Paying Guest Owners & Paying Guests

One of the most common problems owners of paying guest houses face is keeping track of food count and food preference of the paying guests staying in his or her paying guest house. Similarly, often the paying guests also face many problems related to getting incorrect quantity of food and mismatch in their food preference. These food related issues keep on arising because effective kitchen management of paying guest house owners lack behind. To avoid all these problems, our management company have come up with a new solution. By knowing what supplies the owners have in hand at a given time, owners will be able to plan food menu, change menu items when required for their respective paying guests and put the menu on our website by registering themselves while the paying guests can select their preferred meal from the given menu along with the quantity they want to consume by registering themselves. Our system will generate an unique code for each registered owner of paying guest houses while their respective paying guests will have to use a specific code forwarded to them by their respective owners.

About Us

Who we are?


Based in Kolkata with clients all over India, we are a Food Management link.

What we do?


Replete is an online platform which connects owners of hostels, paying guest houses and tiffin carrier delivery services with their respective hostellers, paying guests and office going clients. It is a food management system which helps the two categories register themselves with us and manage the food related issues on a daily basis. The owners can provide their food menu and also alter the menu as and when required while the hostellers, paying guests and office going clients (i.e. customers), can select the preferred meal from the given menu along with the quantity they want to consume. Our system will generate an unique code for each registered owner of hostels, paying guest houses and tiffin carrier delivery services while their customers will have to use the specific code forwarded to them by their respective owners. Our food managing software stores and updates the data of the owners and customers which helps to keep a track on a regular basis.

Why choose Replete?


You may have heard about various managements systems and among these, you have heard about Restaurant food and menu management system. It is for the first time in India, Replete have come up with a completely new management system, especially for the owners of hostels, paying guest houses and tiffin carrier delivery services and their customers to manage the regular food issues that arises between the owners and the customers which will help to satisfy the hunger of the customers and avoid chaos between the owners and the customers regarding food.
